INRA Stablecoin Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2023

At INRA, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and their personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how it is used, and the measures we take to protect it. By using the INRA Stablecoin, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

In order to provide our services, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information, such as your name, email address, and phone number
  • Financial information, such as your bank account or credit card details
  • Transaction information, such as the details of your transactions and transactions you initiate through our platform
  • Usage information, such as how you use our platform and the frequency and duration of your visits

Use of Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide our services, including executing transactions and managing your account
  • To improve our platform, by analyzing usage patterns and developing new features and products
  • To communicate with you, such as sending you updates and responding to your inquiries
  • To comply with legal requirements, such as anti-money laundering regulations and know-your-customer procedures

Sharing of Information

We will not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in providing our services, such as payment processors and verification providers
  • With law enforcement agencies or other government entities, as required by law or in response to a subpoena
  • In the event of a merger or acquisition, in which case your information may be transferred to the new owner

Security

We take security seriously and have implemented measures to protect the information we collect. These measures include secure servers, encryption of sensitive information, and restricted access to sensitive data by authorized personnel only.

Data Retention

We will retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements. Upon request, we will delete your information from our records.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of the INRA Stablecoin after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@inra.bond
